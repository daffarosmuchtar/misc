To whom it may concern. Or perhaps to those who concern of me in times I heartlessly neglect.

A thought I kept in my mind for a while is that I’ve never actually taken the time to show how I feel or perhaps my appreciation towards how the fact that you—of all the people I meet—are the ones that I could count on. It has come to my knowledge that how I speak myself in my every day is really not how I wish to do so, thus I hope this note finds you well.

Thank you for being my constant reminder that life does not have to be so hard. It is impossible for me to imagine the last six months of my life without the presence of your figure every single day. More than I could try to remember, you have been the couch my ingorant ass could crash onto.

I realize I’m somewhat a difficult person to befriend. I’m weird, awkward, and often insensitive of what I say. The worst part is the fact that I repeatedly goof things off and take you for granted in times no person in their right mind would. But despite all that, you stayed. You managed to make me feel included in your life. I find myself in awe when thinking of how a person—despite all that is already going on with their life—would stay and sit down with a horrible excuse for a friend. In you, I found God’s strongest soldier. Everyday, I am learning from you what it means to mature, to be a friend. To that I am forever grateful.

I hope you find the time to read this, and I hope you know how much you mean to me.
